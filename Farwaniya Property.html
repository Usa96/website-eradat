Farwaniya Property

The Farwaniya property is a commercial building situated on a 1,011 sqm land in Kuwait. It has a significant estimated value of KWD 2,240,000. The building is surrounded by three roads, adding to its accessibility and visibility. It offers various features and facilities, including:

1. Basement: The property includes a basement area that comprises two warehouses. These warehouses provide ample storage space for commercial purposes.

2. Ground Floor: The ground floor of the building serves as the main entrance and reception area. It is designed to create a welcoming and professional atmosphere for visitors.

3. Ten Repeated Floors: The building consists of ten repeated floors, each offering residential spaces. These floors are designed to accommodate four 2-bedroom apartments on each level. The apartments provide comfortable living spaces for residents.

4. Rooftop: The property features a rooftop area, which can be utilized for various purposes. It offers potential for additional amenities such as a rooftop garden, recreational area, or even the possibility of constructing additional floors, depending on local regulations.

Occupancy Rate: The commercial building currently maintains an impressive occupancy rate of 90%, indicating its popularity and demand among tenants or residents.
