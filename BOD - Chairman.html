Chairman of the Board

Dr Sulaiman Tareq Al Abduljader

Dr. Sulaiman is an accomplished professional with a diverse background in finance, investment, and academia. Throughout his career, he has held significant positions in renowned companies and organizations, demonstrating his expertise and commitment to the field. With a strong academic foundation and extensive experience, Dr. Sulaiman has made significant contributions to the finance industry, particularly in the areas of alternative investments, sustainable development, and financial economics

Let's delve into his notable roles and accomplishments:

1. Vice Chairman of Aqar Real Estate Investment Company: Dr. Sulaiman served as the Vice Chairman of Aqar Real Estate Investment Company, a prominent firm listed in Boursa Kuwait with a market value of 80 million USD.
2. Board Member of Al Mal Investment Company: He held a position on the board of Al Mal Investment Company, a financial services company. Additionally, he served as the head of the internal audit committee, ensuring adherence to financial standards and regulations.
3. Vice President of Corporate Finance and Investment Services: Dr. Sulaiman's expertise in finance and investment led him to become the Vice President of Corporate Finance and Investment Services at Coast Investment and Development Company.
4. Advisor to Prominent Institutions: He provided advisory services to esteemed organizations, including the Kuwait Investment Authority (KIA) and the Public Authority of Sports. His insights and guidance contributed to their strategic decision-making processes.
5. Board Member of Kuwait Olympic Committee: Dr. Sulaiman actively participated as a board member of the Kuwait Olympic Committee, playing a vital role in the promotion and development of sports in Kuwait.
6. Academic Contributions: Alongside his professional roles, Dr. Sulaiman has made significant contributions to academia. He chairs the Board of Trustees of RAWAJ Institute and holds the position of Assistant Professor of Finance at Gulf University for Science and Technology (GUST).
7. Research Focus and Achievements: Dr. Sulaiman's research primarily focuses on alternative investments, sustainable development, and financial economics, with an emphasis on emerging and frontier markets. His exceptional work was acknowledged during his time as a Visiting Fellow at the Centre of International Studies (CIS) at the London School of Economics and University of Cambridge. In 2009, he was awarded the Outstanding Thesis Award for his exceptional doctoral research at Latrobe University, Australia.
8. Educational Background: Dr. Sulaiman holds a Bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering and an MBA with a specialization in Finance and Real Estate from the University of Hartford, USA. He further pursued his academic journey, obtaining a PhD in Financial Economics from Latrobe University, Australia.

Through his extensive professional experience, academic contributions, and research achievements, Dr. Sulaiman has demonstrated his commitment to advancing the fields of finance, investment, and academia. His diverse background and expertise continue to make a significant impact in the industry and academic community.
