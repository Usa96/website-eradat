Domus 1 & 2

The Domus property is a staff housing accommodation located in Dubai, UAE, strategically situated close to major tourism hubs. The property boasts a substantial gross floor area of 134,741 sqm, divided into two blocks, and maintains a high occupancy rate of 100%.

Here are the notable features of the building:

1. Two Blocks: The property consists of two blocks, providing ample space to accommodate over 1000 hospitality staff. The blocks are designed to meet the specific housing needs of the staff members, ensuring comfortable living conditions

2. Range of Unit Sizes: Each block comprises 108 units with varying sizes, ranging from 2 to 5 bedrooms per unit. This diverse range of unit sizes allows for flexibility in accommodating staff members with different accommodation requirements.

3. Parking Area: The property offers a dedicated parking area, providing convenient and secure parking spaces for residents' vehicles. This feature adds to the overall convenience and accessibility of the accommodation.

4. Swimming Pool and Gym: The Domus property includes recreational facilities such as a swimming pool and a gym. These amenities offer a means for staff members to relax, exercise, and maintain a healthy lifestyle within the accommodation itself.

5. Recreational Areas: In addition to the swimming pool and gym, the property provides other recreational areas that contribute to a pleasant living environment for the staff members. These areas may include gardens, outdoor seating spaces, or communal gathering areas

The Domus 1&2 property addresses the housing needs of hospitality staff in Dubai, particularly targeting companies within a 25-minute radius. The market for hospitality housing in the area is fragmented, creating an opportunity for the project to establish itself as a preferred choice. The property's close proximity to major tourist destinations further enhances its appeal, offering convenient access to potential hospitality assets and ensuring a vibrant living environment for the staff members.
