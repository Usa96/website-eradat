Overview

ERADAT is a real estate investment company (REIC) that was incorporated in 2006. It was later restructured as a Kuwait Shareholding Real Estate Company in 2010. Its current paid-up capital is KD 13.2 million and operates in the State of Kuwait. The company possesses total assets amounting KD 54.4 million. Its major shareholders are Kuwait Real Estate Company KPSC (AQARAT) and Sanam Real Estate Company KPSC.

ERADAT invests in key properties in Kuwait and Dubai, with further expansion into the GCC region. Its initial portfolio consists of medical, staff housing, and commercial properties. The company's objective is to generate exceptional returns and utilize its expertise to invest in sustainable real estate projects. Ultimately, ERADAT aims to establish a prominent presence in the global real estate market.
