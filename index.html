Expert Management

ERADAT is a real estate investment company (REIC) that was incorporated in 2006. It was later restructured as a Kuwait Shareholding Real Estate Company in 2010. Currently, it has a paid-up capital of KD 12.7 million and operates in the state of Kuwait. The company possesses total assets amounting to KD 54.4 million. Its major shareholders include Sanam Real Estate and Aqarat - Kuwait Real Estate Company.

ERADAT has ambitious plans to invest in key properties in Kuwait and Dubai, with further expansion into the GCC region. Its initial portfolio will consist of medical, staff housing, and commercial properties. The company's objective is to generate exceptional returns and utilize its expertise to invest in sustainable real estate projects. Ultimately, Eradat aims to establish a prominent presence in the global real estate market.

Properties

Durrar Complex

Durrar complex in Kuwait's Riggae area is a commercial building comprising four blocks, totaling a built-up area of 250%. It boasts a high occupancy rate of 90% and offers a basement, ground floor with a guard's room and store, along with six floors featuring eight 1-bedroom apartments each, equipped with a living room, kitchen, and bathroom.

Domus 1

Domus property in Dubai offers staff housing close to tourism hubs with 108 units ranging from 2 to 5 bedrooms, accommodating over 1000 hospitality staff, and featuring amenities like parking, swimming pool, gym, and recreational areas.

Fintas Complex

The Fintas Complex in Kuwait, located in Fintas at a prominent corner, comprises four blocks. With a built-up area (BUA) equivalent to 250%, it boasts a high occupancy rate of 95%. This commercial building features a basement, ground floor, and five floors, accommodating six apartments per floor. It offers excellent visibility and accessibility, making it ideal for a range of commercial purposes.
