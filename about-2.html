About ERADAT

BRIDGING the INVESTMENT DIVIDE in MIDDLE EAST

ERADAT is managed by two well-established and experienced real estate companies, Sanam Real Estate and Kuwait Real Estate Company (AQARAT). Sanam Real Estate was incorporated in 1982, and Kuwait Real Estate Company (AQARAT) was the first real estate company to be incorporated into the Kuwait Stock Exchange. Together, their expertise and resources are combined to deliver exceptional investment opportunities for our clients.
